YOUR DETECTIVE
TOOLKIT
6 skills. unlimited power.
These are the skills that separate a great detective from someone who just guesses. Master them all.
[1] ANALYSE

Tear apart any argument. Find the facts hiding underneath the noise. Ask: what is ACTUALLY being said here?

[2] EVALUATE

Not all evidence is created equal. Learn to spot the difference between solid proof and someone's hot take.

[3] INFER

Read between the lines. Great detectives connect dots that others miss completely.

[4] EXPLAIN

Your thinking is only powerful if you can defend it. Prove your reasoning out loud.

[5] INTERPRET

Every story has a teller. Ask WHO is speaking, and WHY. That changes everything.

[6] SELF-CORRECT

The smartest move? Admitting when you were wrong and leveling up your thinking. No ego. Just growth.
Every puzzle you solve reveals WHY you were right or wrong. The explanation IS the reward. The more you understand, the more powerful you become.
